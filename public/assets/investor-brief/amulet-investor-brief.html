Product Demos

Landing Experience

Landing Page

A modern, conversion-optimized landing page that introduces users to the Amulet platform. Features a clean hero section with clear value proposition, interactive demo of the AI chat functionality, and seamless onboarding flow. Users can connect their wallet or sign up with email to get started.

Authentication

Multi-Auth Login System

Flexible authentication supporting both traditional email/password login and Web3 wallet connection via RainbowKit. Users can choose their preferred authentication method, with seamless session management and secure token handling. Supports multiple wallet providers including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and WalletConnect.

E-Commerce

Supplement Marketplace

A curated catalog of longevity-focused supplements with detailed product information, pricing, and direct add-to-cart functionality. Products are organized by category (cognitive, sleep, energy, etc.) with AI-powered recommendations based on user health goals. Integrated with Stripe for secure payment processing.

Monetization

Credit Purchase System

Users can purchase compute credits to access AI health consultations. Multiple credit packages available with volume discounts. Seamless Stripe checkout integration with automatic credit delivery. Credits can also be earned through staking tokens or claiming free monthly allocations.

Telehealth

Doctor Visit Tracking

Comprehensive telehealth integration allowing users to schedule, track, and review their medical visits. Full visit history with doctor notes, prescriptions, and follow-up recommendations. Integrated health questionnaires prepare patients for consultations and ensure doctors have relevant context.

Engagement

Rewards & Leaderboard

Gamified rewards system that incentivizes platform engagement. Users earn points through AI consultations, purchases, and referrals. Real-time leaderboard displays top users with achievements and tier levels. Social proof elements drive viral growth and community building.
